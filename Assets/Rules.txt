Veggies:	A dish must not have exactly 3 vegetables

Fruits:		A dish must always have more vegetables than fruits

Meats: 		Each dish must have exactly 1 meat



Carrots:	A dish must not have more than 5 carrots

Potatoes:	If a dish contains potatoes, there must be an equal number of carrots

Tomatoes:	There must always be an odd number of tomatoes

Lemon:		Lemon cannot be paired with potato

Chicken:	In a dish with chicken, there must be at least 2 carrots for each tomato

Beef:		A dish with beef may have either tomato or lemon, not both
